- **Due:** Apr 12 (available until Apr 14)
- **Points:** 100
- **Format:** File upload
- **Requirements:**
  - **TWO parts:** (1) Research narrative 750+ words, (2) Bibliography of 10+ potential sources
  - **Research narrative sections:**
