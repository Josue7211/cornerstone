# Research Brief: AI Hardware Architecture Evolution & Democratization
Started: 2026-03-29

## Research Question
How has GPU architecture evolved from graphics rendering to AI acceleration, what differentiates CPU/GPU/TPU/NPU architectures, and what does specialized AI hardware mean for the future of computing and equitable access to AI?

## Scope
- **In scope:** GPU evolution (GeForce 256 through Blackwell), CUDA's pivotal role, TPU systolic arrays vs GPU SIMT, NPU emergence for on-device AI, democratization of AI through consumer hardware, local AI movement
- **Out of scope:** AI algorithms/software frameworks, specific AI applications, detailed transistor physics, pricing/business strategy analysis
- **Domain:** Computer engineering / hardware architecture / AI systems
